    • The Irresistible EdikaIkong Soup

    Ingredients

    1. 1kg Spinach/fluted Pumpkin leaves(Ugwu)
    2. 200g Water leaves
    3. 500g of assorted meat(cow tripe (shaki),snails, cowâ€™s skin(kanda),
    4. Stockfish
    5. Smoked fish
    6. 250ml palm oil
    7. 1 cup Periwinkles(optional), but it gives the soup a boost
    8. 2 tablespoons ground crayfish
    9. 2 stock cubes
    10. 1 medium sized onion
    11. 2 scotch bonnet Pepper a.k.a fresh pepper
    12. 2 scotch bonnet Pepper a.k.a fresh pepper

    Steps

    Step1

    Prepare the vegetables by washing and cutting them into tiny bits(thin slices preferably) and set aside. Wash and cut the assorted meat in bite-able sizes. Clean the periwinkles, dried fish and stock fish and set aside.

    Step2

    Place the assorted meat and stock fish in the pot , add a little water,the chopped onion and one stock cube.Cook until all the meat is properly cooked and there is just a little liquid in the pot(remember to cook the toughest meat first).

    Step3

    When the meat is done, add the dried fish and periwinkle.Add the palm oil, the ground pepper and crayfish and leave to boil for about 10 minutes.

    Step4

    Now add the water leaves(or lamb lettuce) and leave to cook for 3 minutes. Then add the Ikong Ubong /Ugu leaves(fluted pumpkin leaves) and salt to taste. Stir thoroughly and leave to simmer for 5 minutes on low heat and your Edikang Ikong soup is ready. Enjoy with any swallow e.g Pounded Yam!
